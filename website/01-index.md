# Greetings, dear visitor of the Oracle DBA site!

<br/>

This resource collects notes related to the administration of Oracle databases and closely related technologies (Oracle Database, Weblogic, Oracle Golden Gate, etc.).

<br/>

Notes may contain errors; as they are found and as new knowledge emerges, the content will be corrected.

Unfortunately, I cannot publish all materials, so the site contains examples of a fairly general nature.

If you find an error, write to me, we'll fix it!

Critical comments? Yes, please, but keep it constructive.

<br/>

![Marley](/img/a3333333mail.gif 'Marley')

<br/>

P.S. I moved to development quite a while ago, mainly Java (Oracle ADF / JET framework), but sometimes I still have to do something in SQL and PL/SQL. As a result, I haven't upgraded my administration skills for a long time, and I've forgotten a lot. But I still understand the basics. Don't count on me adding something here that I don't work with on a daily basis.

But you can share your knowledge; in return, I can post a link to your LinkedIn, share training materials. Maybe even give some advice.
