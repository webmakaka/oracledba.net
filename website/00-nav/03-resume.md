# Post your resume on our site

I suggest that people who want (if any are found) to share their experience with visitors of our site place a link to their resume here.

If desired, it could be a link to a job search site, a social network account, or a linkedin profile.

You can also specify, for example, in your linkedin profile that you are participating in this project.

What are the conditions?  
Write 5 articles (not necessarily large ones) and post them on our site.

What materials might be interesting?  
Performance tuning, weblogic administration, Enterprise Manager, SOA, RPAS, GoldenGate, ATG, PeopleSoft, BPM, BI, Hudson, Oracle HTTP Server features, Oracle Linux, Oracle VM, Jdeveloper, SQL Developer, and much more that I don't know about or haven't remembered.

If interested, join the chat or write to the email address.

Not everyone is satisfied with their current job and compensation. Maybe this will somehow help you change something in your life.

The site is visited mainly by people from the capitals of the largest CIS states. Maybe there will be those who want to move to the capital region, where salaries are higher and toys are more interesting.
